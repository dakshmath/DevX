Our Story

Innova was founded by two tech enthusiasts from Mt. Hebron High School with a shared passion for web design and a desire to help local businesses. We saw that many small businesses in our community in Ellicott City, Maryland didn't have an online presence or were struggling with outdated websites that didn't represent their brand properly.

We understand the importance of having a professional online presence in today's market. We decided to combine our technical skills with our entrepreneurial spirit to create modern websites for local businesses.

Our mission is simple: to help small businesses thrive in the digital world by providing them with beautiful, functional websites that connect them with more customers.
